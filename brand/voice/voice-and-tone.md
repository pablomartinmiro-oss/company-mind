# Voice & Tone — Company Mind

## The one-line positioning
An AI CRM that replaces spreadsheet chaos with calm, trusted clarity.

## Five voice rules
1. **Direct, not clever.** "Call needs review" beats "Oops, we hit a snag."
2. **Numbers before adjectives.** "3 calls over 90s today" beats "a few strong calls."
3. **Operator tone, not customer service.** Talk like a peer running ops with the user, not a chatbot trying to help.
4. **Never apologize for AI limitations in product copy.** Show what the AI knows with confidence, or stay silent.
5. **Short sentences. Shorter buttons.** Max 3 words on any CTA.

## Copy examples

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| "Oops! Something went wrong." | "Couldn't load calls. Retry." |
| "Great job! You've completed a task!" | "Task done." |
| "Our AI is analyzing your call..." | "Scoring..." |
| "Welcome back, user!" | "Good morning, Pablo." |
| "No items to display" | "No calls yet." |
| "Click here to add your first contact" | "Add contact" |
